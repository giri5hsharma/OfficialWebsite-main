Our Journey

January 2024

Club Formation

The club was officially formed with a vision to create a thriving community of tech enthusiasts.

April 2024

FOSS in AI

An event exploring AI and open source, featuring a guest speaker, held on April 19th at Symbiosis Institute of Technology Pune.

July 2024

FOSS Hack 2024

The club co-hosted FOSSHACK 2024 with AIML SIT Pune on July 27th & 28th, focusing on innovation and open-source development.

July 2024

Sponsored OpenWRT Project

The club worked on porting OpenWRT to Archer A6 V4, enhancing its capabilities with open-source firmware.

February 2025

TechFest Workshop

The club hosted a workshop at TechFest, introducing students to open-source collaboration and real-world applications.
